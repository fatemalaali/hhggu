Saffron chia pudding

This recipe is so filling and so easy to make. You'll find yourself indulging in it regularly for the health benefits that it offers.

About the recipe:

This recipe is so easy to make, I came up with this recipe as a healthier alternative to Sago dish-(Sago dish: A Bahraini dish which is made up of a lot of sugar and sago starch, it has got a similar texture to pudding but it is stickier). Chia seeds, when soaked in water, get a pudding-like consistency, hence why I chose them as the main ingredient.

Ingredients Measurments
Chia seeds 1/4 cup
Saffron 1/8 tsp
Date molasses 2 tbsps
Unsweetened almond milk 3/4 cup
Rose-water 2 tbsp
Palm-water 2 tbsp
Ground cardamom 1 tsp

Instructions:

  1. Mix up all of the ingredients in a jar and leave it in the fridge overnight.
  2. Top it up with whatever toppings you want and eat it.
