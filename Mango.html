Healthy Mango Frozen Yogurt

Try this recipe, and you will simply fall in love with it.

About recipe

I love frozen yogurt, it is one of my favorite treats, however, the store-bought one is full of sugars, food colorings, and other unhealthy ingredients. This is why I came up with this recipe. I thought to myself that the main ingredients of frozen yogurt are the fruit and the yogurt so why not try to create it with only these two ingredients?

Ingredients:

  • - 200 grams of Frozen Yogurt
  • - 400 grams of Frozen Mangos
  • - 80 grams of Non-frozen yogurt
  • - 1 tbsp or more of any sweetener(optional, I would only add a sweetner if the mangoes are not sweet enough. It also dependes on how sweet you like it.)

Instructions:

  • Slice up the mangos and freeze them overnight
  • Distribute the yogurt on a silicon matt and freeze it overnight.
  • Mix up all of the ingredients in a food processor.
  • To store it, keep it in an air tight container in the freezer.
