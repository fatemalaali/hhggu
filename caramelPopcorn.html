Healthy caramel popcorn

About recipe:

This recipe is simple to make, and it only requires 5 ingredients. On top of that, it is a healthier option than the conventional caramel popcorn as butter is replaced with coconut oil and white sugar with unrefined coconut sugar. While it's true that it is not low in calories or sugar, it is still a better option than regular caramel popcorn.

Ingredients:

  1. Caramel ingredients:
    1. 1/4 cup of coconut sugar
    2. 1 tsp of coconut oil
    3. 2 tbsp of unsweetened vanilla almond milk
    4. pinch of sea salt
  2. 1/3 cup of Popcorn seeds

Recipe steps:

  1. make the caramel:
    1. Heat coconut sugar on a pan on medium-high heat while stirring it for a minute
    2. Pour coconut oil & almond milk on the sugar
    3. Stir the caramel for a minute
    4. Turn of the heat
  1. Make the popcorn. I use a popcorn machine to make it. If you don't own one you could cook the popcorn in a pot.
  2. Mix the popcorn with the caramel.
