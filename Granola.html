Granola Recipe

About Recipe:

This is my favorite recipe on this website, It is one of the best granola recipes that I've encountered, my cousin shared this recipe with me and I have been enjoying it ever since! I told my cousin that I would publish this recipe on my website and she was okay with it. This granola paired with yogurt is my favorite breakfast to eat.

Ingredients Measurments
Rolled oats 2 cups
Raisins 1/3 cup
Pistachios(Any kind of nuts would work, but I prefer pistachios) 1/3 cup
Pumpkin seeds or chopped almonds(Optional) 1/4 cup
Rose petals(Optional) 1 tbsp
Ground cardamom 1 tsp
Unrefined olive oil 1/4 cup
Honey(Any sweetener would work) 1/4 cup(You could add a bit more depending on how sweet you like it)

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 150c.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients. You will have a honey-oil paste.
  4. Mix the dry ingredients with the paste.
  5. Spread the mixture in a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Let it cook in the oven for 20 minutes.
